# Product Overview

This repository contains multiple AI governance and compliance projects focused on enterprise-grade solutions for managing AI systems in regulated environments.

## Core Products

### AI Governance Dashboard
A React-based dashboard for managing AI model compliance and risk assessment. Provides role-based workflows for developers, data protection officers (DPOs), and executives to track AI model compliance according to regulatory frameworks like the EU AI Act.

**Key Features:**
- Global risk overview with compliance gauges
- Model registry with filtering and sorting
- Automated risk classification (High-Risk, Limited, Minimal)
- Role-based access control and workflows
- Interactive demo tour for presentations
- Export functionality for compliance reporting

### AI Compliance Platform
A comprehensive system for operationalizing AI compliance frameworks with dual-mode operation for both self-assessment and regulatory evaluation.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time AI guardrails with content filtering
- Industry-specific compliance rules (Financial Services, Healthcare, Automotive, Government)
- Automated compliance checking and monitoring
- Comprehensive audit trail with regulatory-grade documentation
- FastAPI backend with React frontend

## Target Industries

- **Financial Services**: Banking regulations, insurance compliance, investment services
- **Healthcare**: HIPAA privacy, FDA medical device regulations, clinical trial compliance
- **Government/Public Sector**: Transparency requirements, algorithmic accountability
- **Automotive**: Functional safety standards, autonomous vehicle regulations

## Demo and Presentation Ready

Both platforms include comprehensive demo capabilities with realistic sample data, interactive tours, and professional presentation modes suitable for stakeholder demonstrations and regulatory discussions.
