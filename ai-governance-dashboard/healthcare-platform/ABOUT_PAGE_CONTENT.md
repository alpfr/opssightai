# About Page Content Guide

## Overview
The About page (`/about`) tells the Vantedge Health story with a focus on solving the "Small Practice Squeeze" and returning humanity to healthcare.

## Content Structure

### 1. Hero Section
**Headline**: "Returning Humanity to Healthcare"
**Subheadline**: Brief mission statement about using Agentic AI to solve the Small Practice Squeeze

### 2. The Origin Story: Why We Exist
**The Marcus Scenario**: A real-world example of fragmented patient care
- Simple knee injury
- 14 days and 20 phone calls to resolve
- Multiple handoffs, missed calls, wrong departments
- Illustrates the broken patient journey

**Key Message**: This fragmentation breaks both patients and providers

### 3. The Problem: Small Practice Squeeze
- Independent clinics trapped between rising costs and burnout
- Doctors spend more time on logistics than medicine
- Staff overwhelmed with administrative tasks
- Result: Providers working until 9 PM, patients falling through cracks

### 4. Our Philosophy: The 3 Pillars

#### Pillar 1: Clinical-First AI
- **Icon**: Shield (security/protection)
- **Message**: We don't replace doctors—we protect their time
- **Focus**: AI handles logistics, physicians handle medicine

#### Pillar 2: Closed-Loop Care
- **Icon**: Users (connection/community)
- **Message**: No patient falls through the cracks
- **Focus**: Track every handoff, confirm every appointment, alert on follow-up needs

#### Pillar 3: RADICAL Transparency
- **Icon**: Heart (care/ethics)
- **Message**: Ethics-led AI that is HIPAA-native and provider-approved
- **Focus**: Explainable decisions, protected data, auditable algorithms

### 5. The 2026 Edge
**Headline**: "This is the next evolution of the medical office"

**Key Features**:
- **Role-Based Triage**: AI routes patients based on clinical need, insurance, and availability
- **Smart Logistics**: Traffic-aware appointment reminders with real-time leave times
- **Predictive Capacity Planning**: AI forecasts no-shows and optimizes schedules

### 6. Call to Action: Reclaim Your 9:00 PM
**Target Audience**: Clinic owners tired of administrative burden
**Message**: Join the Vantedge network and rediscover why you became a doctor
**CTA Button**: "Schedule a Demo"
**Subtext**: "No sales pitch. Just a conversation about better care."

### 7. Footer
**Credentials**: Vantedge Health • Founded 2026 • HIPAA Compliant • Provider-Approved

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone
- **Grounded but Visionary**: Avoid Silicon Valley buzzwords
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledge provider exhaustion
- **Clinical Efficiency Language**: Use healthcare terminology, not tech jargon

### Writing Style
- Short paragraphs for scannability
- Bullet points for key features
- Headers for clear section breaks
- Under 600 words total

### Visual Language
- **Deep Slate**: Security, professionalism, trust
- **Healing Teal**: Vitality, innovation, care
- **Soft Sage**: Balance, calm, support

## Content Updates & Maintenance

### When to Update
- New features or capabilities added
- Company milestones reached
- Customer success stories available
- Industry recognition or awards
- Regulatory compliance updates

### What to Preserve
- The Marcus scenario (core story)
- The 3 Pillars framework
- Brand voice and tone
- Under 600 words constraint
- Glanceable format

## Accessibility Considerations

- Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
- Icon + text labels for all sections
- Sufficient color contrast (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader friendly content
- Mobile-responsive design

## Performance Metrics to Track

- Page views and time on page
- Scroll depth (how far users read)
- CTA click-through rate
- Bounce rate
- Mobile vs desktop engagement
- Referral sources
